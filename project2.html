DULCE VICIO PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Individual Project

Since Nov 2018 ~ From Dec 2018

Dulce Vicio is cafeteria websites that I made for friend's dad.

Major Technology that I used was HTML, SASS and JavaScript. From google searching, I found out those colors that I used have some psychological effects. For instance, warmer color like orange (color code #f07818, and #f0a830) evoke emotions ranging from feelings of warmth and comfort to feelings of anger and hostility. Cool color like green (#2a5c0b, and #808f12) are often described as calm, but can also call to mind feelings of appetite. So I decided to use them basic color.

I payed more attention to make simple and neat but also not looks simple and easy.
